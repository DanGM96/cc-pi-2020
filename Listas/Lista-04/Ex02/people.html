People

As of December 4, 2019, a total of 565 people from 41 countries have gone into space according to the FAI criterion (574 people have qualified when including the US Department of Defense classification). Of the 565, three people completed a sub-orbital flight, 562 people reached Earth orbit, 24 traveled beyond low Earth orbit and 12 walked on the Moon. Space travelers have spent over 29,000 person-days (or a cumulative total of over 77 years) in space including over 100 person-days of spacewalks.
